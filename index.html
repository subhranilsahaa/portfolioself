About

Hi, I'm Subhranil, an 18 year old Computer Science student from Kolkata who spends an impressive amount of time convincing computers to cooperate with me and an even more impressive amount of time figuring out why they suddenly stopped cooperating five minutes later. I build websites, develop Android applications, and explore cybersecurity, mostly because apparently sleeping eight hours a day is optional in this industry. My interests range from full stack development and UI design to penetration testing and learning how things work beneath the surface. When I'm not writing code, I'm usually debugging something that broke because of a typo, a missing semicolon, or a CSS property that decided to interpret "center" as a philosophical concept rather than an instruction. My development process generally involves optimism, confusion, stack overflow, confidence, panic, and eventually success. I enjoy building projects that are fast, functional, and visually appealing. My goal is simple: keep learning, keep building, and someday write code that works on the first try just to experience what that feels like.
